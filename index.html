Welcome to my corner of the internet! I am Atri Mishra, though you can call me Akash.

If you were to sum me up in a single equation, it would be equal parts quantum mechanics and cricket strategy. Yes, you read that right — I have Physics in my blood and a cricket bat in my hand.

The Academic Trajectory

  • Schooling: Completed up to 12th grade at Ashok Singh.
  • Graduation: B.Sc. in Physics (Hons.) from Kirori Mal College, DU.
  • Post-Graduation: Pursuing M.Sc. in Physics from the prestigious Hindu College (NIRF Rank 1).

Beyond the classroom, I am a builder at heart. I served as the Head Member of the Robotics Society from 2023 to 2025, where I spent my time bringing machines to life and figuring out how to automate the world.

On the Pitch

When I'm not analyzing data, you'll find me on the cricket field. I am a cricket lover and have a winner's trophy in my cabinet to prove it. I have always been the captain of my cricket teams, leading various squads over the years to a stellar winning percentage of 87%.

A Quick Disclosure

Despite leading teams and societies, in the social equation of life, I currently consider myself a friendless quantity. I am still searching for that perfect "best friend" molecule to complete my bond.

Proudly raised by my wonderful parents, Shri Raj Mani Mishra and Smt. Amita Mishra.
